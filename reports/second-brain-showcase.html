The idea in one line

Keep the shoebox. Also write the summary.

Years of my own data — ChatGPT chats, Garmin health history, Instagram saves, Netflix, Google Takeout — sit underneath as a searchable archive. On top, an AI distills them into clean, linked notes I can actually find. I decide what matters; it does the reading, summarizing, linking, and bookkeeping.

The part I didn't expect to love

It also runs all my projects

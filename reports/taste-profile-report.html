The Chick-fil-A monument

210 orders · ~$2,088

Chick-fil-A is the whole personality — more spend than the next four favorite spots combined. The order barely changes: Chick-n-Strips (his #1 item, ordered 161 times) plus Waffle Fries. The throughline across every cuisine is chicken — strips, sesame chicken, Greek chicken pita, fried-chicken rice bowls.

Repetitive within, adventurous across: he'll try Cuban, Argentine, ramen, and Greek — but once he finds the dish, he orders it on a loop.

What he actually cooks

High-protein bowls

The home-cooking pattern mirrors the eating-out one — protein-forward, bowl-shaped. Street-corn chicken bowls, cilantro-lime chicken bowls (a recreation of the Just Salad version), and honey-garlic steak noodles saved with macros (~566 cal / 59g protein per serving). The nutrition rule of thumb he's internalized: carbs energize, protein satisfies, both do both.

A real Taiwanese street-food streak

Beyond the protein bowls, a clear sub-interest: Taiwanese popcorn chicken ("one of the best fried chicken recipes in the world") and scallion pancakes that recur again and again — the green-onion-log dough technique, night-market versions, even a scallion-pancake BEC. The same Taiwan pull that shows up in his travel shows up in his kitchen.

Host mode

Two Friendsgivings in one season, a Costco smoked turkey with a paprika-thyme compound butter, pizza-stone-on-the-grill, and vegetarian date-night menus built for Crissy (grilled halloumi, air-fryer fries, chocolate-dipped strawberries).

Things the food data quietly knew

No sweet tooth. Dessert and boba are a combined ~1% of orders. The indulgence is fried, not sugary.
Delivery is work fuel, not a vice. Only 17 of 1,495 orders in nine years ever landed after 10pm.
The palate matured on a curve. Average order climbed from ~$7 pandemic snack-runs (2021) to ~$25–30 real meals by 2024 as ramen and healthy bowls rose.
The order history doubles as a move log. The delivery address trail — Short Pine → Elizabeth Rose → Paddle Ct — traces three successive Orlando homes.
He credits service by name. Reviews praise staff like Emma and Josue personally — he notices who took care of him.

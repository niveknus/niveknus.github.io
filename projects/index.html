Instead of one-off chats, every conversation feeds into a structured knowledge base, project tracker, and suite of AI agents that actively manage parts of my life. The AI does all the bookkeeping, cross-referencing, and maintenance that humans give up on.

How It All Connects

The Second Brain is the backbone. Every project has a wiki page that cross-links to other topics. The Fitness Coach reads biometric data and writes to the wiki. The Personal Assistant manages tasks and calendar. The Life Coach will track goals that span across projects. The Home Server will host it all locally.


Claude remembers everything between sessions because the wiki and project files are the memory. No chat history needed — Claude reads the project dashboard, picks up where it left off, and updates everything when done. It's a system that gets smarter the more you use it.
